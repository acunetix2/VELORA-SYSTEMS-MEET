## 🌟 The Vision: What is Velora Meet?

Velora Meet is a next-generation video conferencing platform built on a fundamental belief: **collaboration should be frictionless.** 

We designed Velora Meet to strip away the bloat, the forced updates, and the privacy concerns that plague traditional meeting tools. We believe that joining a meeting should be as simple as clicking a link—no downloads, no forced sign-ups, and no waiting for an app to install. 

Powered by intelligent, built-in AI and secure peer-to-peer architecture, Velora Meet provides crystal-clear audio and video without sacrificing your device's performance or your personal privacy. Whether you are hosting a quick 1-on-1 catchup or a massive company all-hands, Velora ensures that the focus remains entirely on your conversation, not on the software powering it.

## ✨ Comprehensive Features

Velora Meet is packed with powerful features that remain hidden until you need them, maintaining a completely clean and distraction-free UI.

### 🌐 Core Meeting Experience
- **Browser-First Architecture:** Join instantly from Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox. Zero installations, extensions, or plugins are required.
- **Adaptive Quality Protocol:** Seamlessly adjust video and audio quality based on your active network conditions. You stay connected and clear even on low-bandwidth networks, such as hotel Wi-Fi or cellular connections.
- **Auto-Scaling Video Grid:** Our dynamic participant layout automatically adjusts to fit any screen size, smoothly pinning active speakers to create the perfect viewing experience.

### 🧠 Velora AI Intelligence Hub
- **Your Built-in Assistant:** Velora AI sits quietly in the background, ready to assist. It analyzes context, answers questions, and generates insights without ever sending your sensitive audio to third-party cloud servers.
- **Automated Summaries:** Instantly generate meeting recaps, extract critical action items, and distribute bulleted notes the moment the call ends.
- **Live Captions:** Real-time, on-device processing provides incredibly accurate captions that are both lightning-fast and completely private.

### 🤝 Seamless Collaboration Tools
- **High-Fidelity Screen Sharing:** Instantly share your entire screen, a specific application window, or a single browser tab in crisp, uncompressed high definition.
- **Non-Disruptive Engagement:** Communicate without interrupting the active speaker. Utilize built-in emoji reactions, a dedicated "raise hand" feature, and private chat functionality.
- **Host Controls:** Utilize intuitive moderation tools, one-click waiting rooms, and secure lobby management to orchestrate professional, uninterrupted calls.

### 🏢 Velora Enterprise Hub
- **Centralized Organization Management:** High-fidelity dashboard for managing multi-team workspaces, billing, and organizational identity.
- **Team Governance:** Department-wise member grouping, manual onboarding dialogs, and strategic leadership (Admin/Associate) role management.
- **Visual Identity:** Personalize your workspace with custom organizational logos and cover banners to project a professional brand image.
- **Telemetry & Analytics:** Real-time monitoring of meeting volume, active seats, and AI utilization metrics synced directly from your workspace.

### 🔒 Uncompromising Security
- **Always-On End-to-End Encryption (E2EE):** Your meetings remain strictly private with E2EE enabled by default—no premium subscriptions required to secure your data.
- **Organization Security Passcode:** Secondary security layer ensuring only authorized members with the organization key can access sensitive workspace assets.
- **Domain-Level Provisioning:** Automatically authorize and onboard team members based on verified corporate email domains.
- **SAML / SSO Ready:** Built-in hooks for enterprise-grade authentication via Okta, Azure AD, or Google Workspace.
- **Privacy by Design:** We don't collect, sell, or analyze your meeting data for advertising.

Using Velora Meet transforms your daily collaboration workflow:
